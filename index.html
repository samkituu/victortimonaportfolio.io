Recent Work

HDPE, PE, AND PVC

Waste HDPE, PE, AND PVC can be recycled by our partners in the making of new usable plastics for various industrial applications.

General Waste Clearance

We help in the clearance of both organic and General waste (garbage).

Bitumen Steel Drums

Steel Drums containing bitumen for asphalt concrete from Iran. They have a capacity of about 190kgs and once flattened out their dimensions are 5x3 ft. They each weigh 10 kg when completely empty. These can be recycled by the informal sector I.e "Jua Kali" in the making of jikos, metallic boxes, fencing of structures, and building houses. Contact us for the disposal or purchase of these or any such items at your construction site.

Timber offcuts and saw dust

Post construction timber offcuts waste from carpentry. This can be recycled or reused depending on their state.

Steel scrap and cast iron

Steel scrap in the form of carbon steel offcuts from reinforcement bars, and cast iron from written off heavy machinery are also recycled by our partners into billets.

Asphalt Concrete

Asphalt concrete removed from ongoing construction of high volume highways can be recycled in four different types of recycling methods: (1) hot recycling; (2) hot in-place recycling; (3) cold in-place recycling; and (4) full depth reclamation.

Used Tyres, and oil

For major infrastructal projects, heavy automotive tyres and used oil accumulate at an alarming rate. We transfer this to our recycling partners.
